Porsche 911 GT3 Less Known Features

1-) Active Spoiler: The Porsche 911 GT3 RS features an active rear spoiler that automatically adjusts its angle to provide optimal downforce and stability at high speeds. The active spoiler helps improve aerodynamics and cornering performance, making the 911 GT3 RS a true track-focused sports car.

2-) Serie Name: At the beginning, "Porsche 901" was planned to be the name of the car in 1960s. However, Peugeot claimed the rights to car names with a "0" in the middle. Therefore, Porsche had to change the name to "Porsche 911".

3-) Roll Cage: The Porsche 911 GT3 RS offers an optional roll cage, which is a safety feature designed primarily for track use. A roll cage is a metal framework integrated into the car’s chassis, and its purpose is to protect the driver and passengers by reinforcing the vehicle’s structure in the event of a crash.
