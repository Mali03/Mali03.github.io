Porsche 911 GT3 RS Engine

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS has 4.0L atmospheric engine.

Details

The engine produces 518 horsepower and generates 465 Nm torque. The Porsche 911 GT3 RS features a 6-cylinder engine in a flat-six configuration. The engine is high-revving with a redline 9,000 RPM. Acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h takes 3.2 seconds, which is incredible. Front-mounted radiators and aerodynamic air vents provide an advanced cooling system to handle extreme conditions during high-performance driving.
